What “2–24h” cash-out means

Many withdrawals land in 2–24 hours. E-wallets are often quickest; banks drag across weekends; crypto waits on chain traffic and reviews. Busy sports nights can queue checks—normal. Licensed operators segregate funds—see MGA or UKGC for how that works in regulated markets.
