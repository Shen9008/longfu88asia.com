What to verify in the live terms (before “claim”)

Headline percentages do not replace the wagering math. In the account promotion panel, confirm:

  • Multiplier — e.g. (deposit + bonus) × N vs. bonus-only × N.
  • Time limit — days to complete rollover before the bonus (and often winnings tied to it) expire.
  • Max bet while wagering — breaching it can void bonus funds.
  • Game weighting — slots often 100%; live/table may be reduced or excluded.
  • Payment method — some rails (certain e-wallets or crypto) may be excluded from specific welcome paths; align with payments.
  • Free-spin mechanics — per-spin value, eligible games, batching schedule if spins are released over several days.

Why we do not paste rollover here

Operators adjust multipliers, caps, and eligibility by market, currency, and campaign. A static number on a marketing site would go stale or be wrong for your region. The authoritative figures are the ones attached to the offer you accept after login.
